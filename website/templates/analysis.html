Analysis of anime

To learn more about the trend of anime, different graphs are generated and shown below.

Graph from google trends

From the google trends graph, it is noticable that anime is unpopular between the year 2004 and 2015. It is noticable that starting from the year 2016, anime is getting more popular . The number of interest in anime raise from 13 to 83 within year 2016, showing that people from worldwide start to gain interest in anime in 2016. The popularity of anime reached its peak at April 2017, where its interest reaches 100.

Average score by year

From the graph above, anime produced in 1970 has the highest score (8.29), and anime produced in 1963 has the lowest score (7.1) over the years. One of the possible reason for the low score of anime in 1963 is that the technology is not sophisticated enough to produce high quality anime. It is noticable that the score of anime production in recent years has been improving. Between the year of 2016 and 2023, the score gradually increase from 7.23 to 7.54.
