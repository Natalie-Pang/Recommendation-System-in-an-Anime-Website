About Us

This website is created to provide a platform for anime lovers to discover anime. Users can learn more about the information of their favourite animes by going to Anime List page. User can also search for animes based on their name and genres. Besides, by going to Recommend users can get a few recommended anime based on the anime they like.


Users can also create an account in Sign Up to access to more features. You can customise your profile by uploading a picture you like as your icon. With an account you can join create dicussion, or even join any discussion in Discussion page. It is a great chance to meet new anime lover friends!
